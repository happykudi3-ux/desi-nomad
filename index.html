The Founder's Story
I travelled Bhutan, Ladakh, Kasol.
And found the same loophole
in every travel website I visited.
🧳
Bhutan. 11pm. Mountain silence. No signal from the agency.
I'd paid ₹42,000 for this Bhutan trip. Had a simple question about tomorrow's trek permits. Their website? Gorgeous. Their bot? Absent. Their WhatsApp? Dead till 9am. I lay awake anxious — and by morning, I was furious. Not at the mountains. At the gap between the experience they sold and the one they delivered.
🔍
Then I saw it in Kasol. And Ladakh. And Manali.
Trip after trip, the same tragedy. A potential customer asks "Is there snow in Manali in December?" at 11pm — and gets nothing. By morning they've booked with someone who answered. They didn't lose a lead. They never knew they had one.
💡
The best trips in India were bleeding leads every single night.
The trips were extraordinary. The guides brilliant. The itineraries meticulously crafted. But between 9pm and 9am, the digital door was locked. Every question left unanswered was a booking that went to a competitor. The problem wasn't the product — it was the silence.
🚀
So I built the thing I wished had existed on every trip.
An AI that knows your Ladakh trip as well as your best guide does. That answers at 2am with the same warmth as your office manager at 2pm. That captures the name, the number, the intent — and hands it to you with your morning chai. Built by someone who's been on both sides of this story.
